## Hi, I'm Umberto

I spent the last 20 years in communication and marketing. Now, past 40, I'm
making a deliberate move into offensive security.

This profile is where I learn in public. I'm not a pentester yet - I'm building
the foundation one lab, one writeup, one certification at a time, and documenting
the process honestly as I go. The wins and the dead ends both end up here.

My approach is methodology over speedrun: I care more about understanding why an
attack works than about capturing a flag fast. That mindset comes straight from
years of breaking down complex problems for a living - different domain, same
habit of asking "how does this actually work?"
